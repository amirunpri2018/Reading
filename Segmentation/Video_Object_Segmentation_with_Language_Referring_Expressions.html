Method

  1. Grounding objects in video by referring expressions

    1. 两个基本元素
      1. box proposal O={oi}i=1MO=\{o_i\}_{i=1}^MO={oi​}i=1M​
      2. textual query QQQ
    2. grounding model 输出一组匹配得分S={si}i=1MS=\{s_i\}_{i=1}^MS={si​}i=1M​
    3. 最高得分的proposal被选择为predicted region
    4. 为解决predicted region在时序上的不稳定性(主要是false negative)，对得分SSS进行重排序
      1. 目的：减少false negative；忽略短时出现的目标
      2. 根据overlap
        s^i=si×(∑j=1,j≠iMri,j×dj×sj/ti,j)\hat s_i = s_i\times(\sum_{j=1,j\neq i}^Mr_{i,j}\times d_j\times s_j/t_{i,j})s^i​=si​×(j=1,j≠i∑M​ri,j​×dj​×sj​/ti,j​)

      ri,jr_{i,j}ri,j​是第i,ji,ji,j个box的IoU
      ti,j=∣fi−fj∣t_{i,j}=|f_i-f_j|ti,j​=∣fi​−fj​∣ 是temporal distance, fff是frame
      djd_jdj​ 是原始的score

  2. Pixel-level video object segmentation (box refinement task)

    1. 产生二值图, concatenate RGB 通道和 optical flow magnitude。形成5通道的输入 (Fusing appearance and motion cues)

    2. Training

      1. 在静态图上训练，采用密集分割标注的数据集
      2. bbox由分割gt产生，训练时随机抖动
      3. 综合光流信息 [63]，减去中值做平均

      subtracting the median motion for each frame and averaging the magnitude of the forward and backward flow.

      1. 放射变换：模拟相机或目标的运动
